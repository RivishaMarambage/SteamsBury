About This Event

Join us for an unforgettable evening of smooth jazz and exceptional coffee. Our Saturday Night Jazz series features talented local musicians in an intimate setting perfect for unwinding after a long week.

This week, we're thrilled to host the "Blue Note" quartet, known for their sophisticated interpretations of jazz standards and original compositions. Their melodic style creates the perfect ambiance for conversation and connection.
